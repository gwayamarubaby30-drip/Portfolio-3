About Me

The Journey

I'm Sumit Shrestha, a dedicated basketball player with an unwavering passion for the game. My journey began on the local courts, where I developed my fundamental skills and discovered my love for competition.

Known for my speed and court awareness, I bring a complete game to every match. My playing style combines sharp ball handling with accurate shooting and solid defensive capabilities, making me a versatile and reliable presence on the court.

Every day is an opportunity to improve. Whether it's perfecting my three-point shot, enhancing my defensive footwork, or studying game film, I'm committed to continuous growth as an athlete and teammate.
